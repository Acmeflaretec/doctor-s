What We Do

Our Physiotherapy & Fitness Services

At Doctor's Fitness Studio & Physiotherapy Clinic, we offer specialized physiotherapy and fitness services designed to enhance your health, prevent injuries, and improve your quality of life. Our experts use evidence-based practices to help you recover and stay active.

Neuro Physiotherapy

Focused on neurological conditions like stroke, Parkinson's disease, and more to improve motor function and mobility.

Sports Physiotherapy

Rehabilitation and injury prevention for athletes to maintain peak physical performance.

Cardiac & Pulmonary Physiotherapy

Specialized therapy for heart and lung conditions to improve respiratory function and overall fitness.

Pediatric Physiotherapy

Physiotherapy for children to address developmental delays, congenital conditions, and more.

Post-Operative Physiotherapy

Helping you recover faster and regain function following surgery.

Women's Health Physiotherapy

Specialized therapy for women addressing pelvic floor health, prenatal and postnatal care.

Posture & Ergonomics Physiotherapy

Improving posture and workplace ergonomics to prevent injuries and discomfort.

Geriatric Physiotherapy

Care focused on elderly patients to improve mobility, balance, and overall health.

Orthopedic Physiotherapy

Focused on musculoskeletal conditions to restore function, strength, and mobility.
